## The Vision: The Crayon Colors Global Basic Income (CCGBI) Initiative (formerly Global Stewardship Reconciliation Trust - GSRT)

### 2.1 Core System Architecture

The Crayon Colors Global Basic Income (CCGBI) Initiative (formerly Global Stewardship Reconciliation Trust - GSRT) transforms environmental restoration into a structured economic engine through a five-level color-coded progression:

1.  **White Level:** Basic sorting and neighborhood cleanup (universal entry point)
2.  **Blue Level:** Waterway restoration (rivers, lakes, shorelines)
3.  **Green Level:** Terrestrial ecosystem restoration (forests, soil, land)
4.  **Black Level:** Hazardous materials handling (certified, specialized)
5.  **Gold Level:** Master ecosystem restoration (large-scale, multi-year projects)

### 2.2 Foundational Principles

*   **Work AI Cannot Do:** Physical presence in unstructured environments, complex judgment, community trust, and embodied wisdom
*   **Neurochemical Alignment:** Designed to generate dopamine (achievement), oxytocin (bonding), serotonin (confidence), and regulated cortisol
*   **Universal Accessibility:** Zero-barrier entry with progression based on skill development
*   **Economic Dignity:** Payment for verified work, not passive welfare

### 2.3 Historical Precedent

The model mirrors the 19th-century sanitation revolution where private entrepreneurs built fortunes and essential infrastructure by solving urban waste crises, with municipalities as anchor customers. The Crayon Colors Global Basic Income (CCGBI) Initiative (formerly Global Stewardship Reconciliation Trust - GSRT) applies this proven economic pattern to the 21st-century planetary environmental crisis.
