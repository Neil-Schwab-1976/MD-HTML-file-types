The core CBT model (Beck et al., 1993). Tap any link in the chain to explore it. Use this to trace any moment — craving, conflict, reaction — back to its origin.
① TRIGGER
The activating event — what happened in the outside world or inside your body
↓
② AUTOMATIC THOUGHT
The fast, reflexive thing your mind says — so quick you mistake it for fact
↓
③ FEELING

↓

THE GAP — Where Choice Lives
Between ② and ③ there is a space. That space is where CBT intervenes. You are not your thought. You are the one watching the thought. In that gap — however small — you have a choice.
